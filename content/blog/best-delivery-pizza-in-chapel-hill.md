### Italian Pizzeria III

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-2.jpeg)

First off we have the Chapel Hill classic: **Italian Pizzeria III**. They’re a town favorite and we at Takeout Central feel the same.

You can get a 14'’ or 16'’ pizza of all types. We suggest the Reginella Bianca pizza with Pomodoro a filetto, prosciutto, basil and parmigiano. If veggies are more your vibe though, they have a Veggie Pizza full of staples like mushrooms, green peppers, spinach, tomatoes and onion.

Italian Pizzeria III is also a top location for authentic Italian cuisine; don’t be surprised if you start browsing for pizza and wind up ordering baked ziti for dinner tonight!

[Start your order for tonight here!](https://www.takeoutcentral.com/restaurants/delivery/italian-pizzeria-iii-chapel-hill/17)

### Carrboro Pizza Oven

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-3.gif)

Next up we have the nearby **Carrboro Pizza Oven**. The sizes here range from 14'’, 16'’ to Sicilian with a more contemporary topping option selection.

Toppings go from a contemporary BBQ Chicken to a Grandma pizza for the Italian classic. You’ll also find a delicious ‘Eat your veggies’ pizza topped with black olives, mushrooms, yellow onions, green peppers, mozzarella cheese for the veggie lovers.

Just like Italian Pizzeria III, they also serve up fresh Italian food as well, be sure to check it out while you’re building your cart.

[Order Carrboro Pizza Oven now!](https://www.takeoutcentral.com/restaurants/delivery/carrboro-pizza-oven-carrboro/6/)

### Midici Italian Kitchen

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-5.gif)

Our tour of Chapel Hill Italian restaurants leads us next to **Midici Italian Kitchen**. They specialize in Neapolitan Pizza famously known for its chewy, crisp dough and topped with all-natural ingredients.

Midici really takes care with their ingredients. Using all natural ingredients, often Non-GMO and even offering a gluten-free crust, their care really comes through in each bite.

The Neapolitan pizza size means you can either eat it as a personal pizza or swapped out at your dinner table with friends or a loved one. They recommend 2–3 toppings due to the size but you can trust they’ll be packed full of flavor!

[Order your Neapolitan Pizza here!](https://www.takeoutcentral.com/chapel-hill/restaurants/delivery/midici-italian-kitchen-chapel-hill/1421/)

### Lotsa

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-6.gif)

**Lotsa** is highly personalizable and shockingly fast. They cook their 11'’ pizzas in only 150 seconds with their stone fire ovens so we can deliver it ASAP!

This is the place if you want all of the toppings, aside from a few premium toppings, everything you want to add is free. You could build a whole salad on your pizza with the range of toppings here! Lotsa offers a few signature options such as a meat lovers Carnivore pizza stacked with pepperoni, Italian sausage and crumbled meatballs and some new favorites like Buffalo Chicken pizza and a Chicken Bacon ranch pizza. Lotsa also has gluten free crust for those with gluten allergies or an intolerance.

If you and your group can’t decide on toppings but you know you want all of them AND fast, this is the place for you. You won’t be disappointed.

[Order Lotsa Now!](https://www.takeoutcentral.com/chapel-hill/restaurants/delivery/lotsa-stone-fired-pizza-chapel-hill/1285/)

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-7.gif)

**Benny Cappella’s** is home to the Virginia Slice. This is definitely the biggest pizza you’ll find in Chapel Hill.

If you have a big family or even just a big appetite, [Takeout Central](https://www.takeoutcentral.com) will deliver their 28'’ cheese pizza for only $30. If you don’t want to go all out on a 28'’ pizza, they also sell by the slice and you can only imagine how big that slice is!

### Mellow Mushroom

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-8.gif)

**Mellow Mushroom**, the well known chain is home to great pizza and a great vibe. This place specializes in pizza and the products around it. You can get a calzone, a pizza and a hot sandwich and call it a night without looking anywhere else.

Mellow Mushroom is one of the only places in town where a hungry eater with dietary restrictions can get both *vegan* cheese and a *gluten free crust*. In addition, they also have tempeh seasoned with teriyaki or buffalo sauce to top the pizza! If meat is more your tastes, their house pizza has pepperoni, sausage, ground beef, ham, Applewood smoked bacon as well a range of vegetables as fresh as can be.

The sizes here are 10'’, 14'’, 16'’ and great for a movie night or a few friends to share. If a pizza isn’t enough, add a starter of their fresh bruschetta too!

[Order Mellow Mushroom Here!](https://www.takeoutcentral.com/chapel-hill/restaurants/delivery/mellow-mushroom-chapel-hill-2/25/)

### Pop’s Pizzeria

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-9.gif)

**Pop’s Pizzeria** is an Italian and Mediterranean specialist in Chapel Hill. They’re serving up fresh, brick oven New York style pizza with the most crisp locally grown ingredients.

Pop’s has small, medium and extra large pizzas with plenty of variety in toppings. There’s a stuffed meat pizza packed full of pepperoni, beef, ham, and bacon for the carnivores out there as well as a stuffed veggie pizza for the veggie lovers. The veggie pizza is full of spinach, broccoli, mushrooms, red onions, tomatoes and green peppers.

Everyone will be satisfied eating at Pop’s Pizzeria with something for the whole family. Branch out with their options of turkey, chicken or steak wraps and the whole family will be delighted!

[Bring home some Pizza from Pop’s!](https://www.takeoutcentral.com/restaurants/delivery/pops-pizzeria-chapel-hill/1226/)

### Alfredo’s Pizza Villa

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-10.gif)

**Alfredo’s Pizza Villa** is the home to great pizza. With an offering of 14'’, 16'’ or 18'’ pizzas with fresh toppings, their pizza is perfect for a hungry family.

Alfredo’s has signature pies ranging from their Ranchero pizza to a Brooklyn pizza containing meatballs, sausage and ground beef. Their topping selection is top notch with a great range of eclectic toppings mixed with fresh local and fresh ingredients.

Alfredo’s is also a great place if you want to switch it up and get a delicious calzone for your lunch tomorrow, no one can fault you for thinking ahead. FYI, this place also has a gluten free crust!

[Order from Alfredo’s Pizza Villa tonight!](https://www.takeoutcentral.com/restaurants/delivery/alfredos-pizza-villa-chapel-hill/1420/)

### Might as Well Bar & Grill

![](/images/blog/best-delivery-pizza-in-chapel-hill/img-12.jpeg)

Last but certainly not least we have **Might As Well**. This one is a true Chapel Hill classic. UNC Students know this place from their late night pizza cravings after games and you’ll definitely remember your first slice from here.

Might As Well offers pizzas ranging from an 11'’ personal pan pizza with your choice of toppings to sharable 16'’ pizzas with their signature toppings. Most notably, they have a Mac N Cheese pizza which would be decadent enough to make any evening great in my books. If you’re on a more simpler path though, they do have a margherita pizza with the freshest basil and tomatoes you’ll find.
